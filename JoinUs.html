Requirements

There are only a handful of requirements to be a member:

  • Try to stay Consistent and participate most meetings.

  • Participate in Most of the tournaments

  • Respect the Advisor, the leaders, and your Opponents

  • Don't Play Checkers

  • Have Fun!!

Activities

----There are many sorts of activities in this club. In General Meetings, Students get to challenge an opponent on an over-the-board game or online game. However some days there are different game plans. In Club Tournaments, students participate in a knockout bracket chess tournament with prizes for the winners. In Challenges, a powerful player is picked and must play against all participating students to see how many games he can win simultaneusly.

---In Varients Students either do a small tournament or a match but instead of normal Chess, a varient is played (eg. 4 Player, Atomic, Duck, 960, Crazyhouse, etc). There are some days where students don't play. In cases where a Student is going to a national or international tournament, there is a field trip where hand picked students go to support the accepted student. There are also free yearly field trips in which students go and meet Famous masters to get some advice.

---Finally, there are guest visit days when masters communicate or teach students about chess either in-person or through zoom. These are the hardest ones to coordinate.
Our leaders have worked hard to all of make this possible. So take part in all of it and have fun.
